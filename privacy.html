Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 May 2026

I run this site myself. I keep it small, I keep your data small, and I don't sell anything to anyone. Here's exactly what happens when you use adham.coach.

What I collect

Three categories, plus some automatic technical information.

  • Your email, if you give it to me. That happens when you sign up for a newsletter (the Field Notes list), download the free guide (the 5-Minute Anxiety Reset), or join a pathway and ask me to email you the next step.
  • Your name and message, if you fill out the contact form on the site.
  • Your pathway progress (which step you completed and when), if you've given me your email for a pathway. I keep it on the server so I can send you the next step at the right time, and so you can pick up on a different device.
  • Anonymous page-view counts via Vercel Web Analytics. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no individual tracking — just totals (how many people opened which page).

What I don't collect

  • Cookies that follow you around the web.
  • Anything from third-party trackers (no Facebook Pixel, no Google Analytics, no ad networks).
  • Your IP address (beyond what Vercel and Supabase log briefly for security).
  • Payment information. If we work together one-on-one, payment is handled by a separate processor and I never see your card details.

What I use it for

  • To send you the things you asked for. If you signed up for a newsletter, I send newsletters. If you joined a pathway, I send pathway reminders.
  • To answer you when you write to me.
  • To understand which essays land, via the anonymous page-view counts.

That's it. I don't sell your email to anyone. I don't share it with anyone. I don't use it for retargeting ads.

Where your data lives

  • Email and pathway progress: in a Supabase database (Postgres, hosted in the US).
  • Email sending: via Brevo (transactional emails, hosted in the EU).
  • Site hosting: Vercel (the page you're reading) and GitHub Pages (mirror).
  • Newsletter list: stored in Brevo.

How long I keep it

Until you tell me to stop. Every email I send has a one-click unsubscribe link in the footer. Once you unsubscribe I stop sending emails immediately, but I keep the row marked "unsubscribed" so I don't accidentally re-add you. If you want me to delete your data entirely, email me at adham@adham.coach and I'll do it within 7 days.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you're in the EU or California (or anywhere with similar laws), you have the right to know what I have about you, to correct it, to take it elsewhere, and to make me delete it. To exercise any of these, just email adham@adham.coach. I'll respond within 30 days, usually faster.

Children

This site is for adults. Don't sign up if you're under 16.

Changes

If I change anything material, I'll update the date at the top and, if you're on my email list, I'll send you a note before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions: adham@adham.coach.
