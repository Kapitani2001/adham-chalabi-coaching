FAQ

Straight answers.

What actually is Time Line Therapy®?+

A structured process for finding the root of a recurring emotional pattern, the first moment your mind learned the response, and releasing the charge held there. It's fast and content-light: we don't need to relive the story in detail. We work with how your mind has it filed, and change it at the source.

Is this therapy?+

No. This is coaching. It's forward-looking and focused on where you're going. If you're working through active trauma or a clinical diagnosis, I'll tell you honestly and refer you to a great therapist before we do anything together.

What if I don't know which pattern to bring?+

That's what the intake form is for. Most people know the loop better than they think. It's the thing they keep promising themselves they'll change. If it's foggy, we'll find it together in the first few minutes. You don't need it perfectly worded.

Can 90 minutes really change anything?+

For a single, well-named pattern, yes, often more than months of circling it. This isn't about fixing your whole life in one sitting. It's about pulling one root cleanly, so the pattern it was feeding loses its grip. You leave with the shift and a recording of how we got there.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?+

The session is non-refundable. When you book, that time is held for you and turned away from someone else. But rescheduling is free and easy: give me 24 hours' notice and we'll move it, no charge, no friction. Life happens. I just ask that you tell me before the slot, not after. Here's the promise that backs it: you'll find resolution and real relief from this pattern. And if you don't, we keep working until you do.

What happens after the session?+

You get the recording within 24 hours and a written next step. There's no upsell waiting at the end. If you want to go deeper afterward, you can ask about ongoing 1:1 work, but plenty of people take one session and run with it. No pressure either way.
