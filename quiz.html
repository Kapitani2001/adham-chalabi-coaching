Where are you on the meaning scale?

The natural state of the spirit, left without meaning, is chaos — just as the natural state of the stomach, left without food, is hunger.

And here is the cruel part: without meaning, suffering has no purpose. So you don't just suffer — you suffer the suffering. Pain you can carry when it points somewhere. Pain that points nowhere is what breaks people.

So it's worth knowing, honestly, where you actually stand — and what to do about it.
