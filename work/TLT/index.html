Why nothing has worked yet

You know the pattern.
You just can't break it.

You've done therapy. Journaled. Meditated. Read the books, attended the workshops, hired the coaches. Something shifted for a while. Then the pattern came back — maybe in a different form, but recognisably the same.

That's not failure. That's the wrong level of intervention.

Most approaches work at the level of the story — understanding why the pattern formed, reframing how you think about it. Useful. But the pattern isn't stored in your narrative mind. It's stored in your timeline, as an unconscious decision your younger self made that made perfect sense then.

Until we go to the root — the specific moment, the specific decision — it keeps running. This is where we go.

How the session works

Four moves. One root pulled.

This is the Time Line Therapy® process — not analysis, not advice. A structured walk from the symptom to its source and back out.

01

Name it

We get specific. Not "I procrastinate" — the exact pattern, the exact feeling, the moment it grips you. You can't release what you won't name.

02

Timeline

We trace it back. Your mind filed the first time you learned this response. We find that moment — the root, not the symptom.

03

Release

We let the old emotion and the old decision go. Not over-analyze it — release it. This is the part that actually moves the needle.

04

New decision

You choose what goes in its place — a decision that fits who you are now, not who you were when you made the first one.

FAQ

Straight answers.

What actually is Time Line Therapy®?+

A structured process for finding the root of a recurring emotional pattern — the first moment your mind learned the response — and releasing the charge held there. It's fast and content-light: we don't need to relive the story in detail. We work with how your mind has it filed, and change it at the source.

Is this therapy?+

No. This is coaching — it's forward-looking and focused on where you're going. If you're working through active trauma or a clinical diagnosis, I'll tell you honestly and refer you to a great therapist before we do anything together.

What if I don't know which pattern to bring?+

That's what the intake form is for. Most people know the loop better than they think — the thing they keep promising themselves they'll change. If it's foggy, we'll find it together in the first few minutes. You don't need it perfectly worded.

Can 90 minutes really change anything?+

For a single, well-named pattern — yes, often more than months of circling it. This isn't about fixing your whole life in one sitting. It's about pulling one root cleanly, so the pattern it was feeding loses its grip. You leave with the shift and a recording of how we got there.

What's your refund and rescheduling policy?+

The session is non-refundable — when you book, that time is held for you and turned away from someone else. But rescheduling is free and easy: give me 24 hours' notice and we'll move it, no charge, no friction. Life happens; I just ask that you tell me before the slot, not after.

What happens after the session?+

You get the recording within 24 hours and a written next step. There's no upsell waiting at the end — if you want to go deeper afterward, you can ask about ongoing 1:1 work, but plenty of people take one session and run with it. No pressure either way.
